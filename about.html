Welcome to touchStartup.com!


touchstartup.com is the premier connecticut portal that provides job posts for job seekers, and experts informations for employers to give an opporutnity which meets their needs. and also it provides lastest news and entertainments from various online medias, which creates social interaction bewteen the communites.

the major goal of the website is to serve the comunity as a buffer by creating a communication link between employers and job seekers which means that,Registered Job Seekers can post their resumes, setup Job Email Alerts, communicate with registered employers, forward job listings to friends, and do much more. Likewise, employers can create Company Profile, post/edit job vacancies, communicate with applicants, screen resumes, etc.

the touchstartup is targeted users are jobseekers, employers and communites who wants to participate in social networking and gets daily updates of our sites.

the site was created for a100 program project, by Gruop 3 team memebrs with in the given project time and demostrared to a100 program evaluators or experts(trainers) and coordinators to get tehnical and ideal feedbacks in order to build a well organized website.
